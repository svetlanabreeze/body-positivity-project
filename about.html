Welcome to our Body Positivity website. We are students from TU Dublin and the website is all about our group project "Body Positivity Awareness".

We are a team of women who would love to spread body positivity to other women and make them comfortable in their body. We would like to build a community that doesn’t shame people for their physical appearance. We want a bond, a happy environment that women can complement each other. 

Our Vox Pop

For our Vox Pop we researched and gathered questions we would like to ask. Each member of our team asked people they know some of the questions and we were very surprised how good they were. We had about twenty minutes of footage, we editted it in Adobe Audition and narrowed it down to about four minutes. You can listen to our Vox Pop by pressing "Play" button below.
